Visit Punjab

Enjoy the culture and food of this vibrant state north of India.

Top three activities to do at Punjab

Visit Golden Temple

Visit the Golden Temple, the holiest site in Sikhism, and experience the vibrant culture and traditions of Punjab.

Visit Jallianwala Bagh

Visit Jallianwala Bagh, a historic garden and memorial of national importance.

Visit Sada Pind

Visit Sada Pind, a cultural village that showcases the traditional Punjabi way of life.

Top three foods to try at Punjab

Saroo Saag

Saroo Saag is a traditional Punjabi dish made with mustard greens and cornmeal.

Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken is a rich and creamy chicken dish that is a popular part of Punjabi cuisine.

Chole Bhature

Chole Bhature is a popular Punjabi dish made with chickpeas and fried bread.

Lassi

Lassi is a traditional Punjabi drink made with yogurt and water.

Amritsari Kulcha

Amritsari Kulcha is a leavened bread stuffed with potatoes, onions, and spices, baked in a tandoor, and typically served with chole (chickpea curry).
